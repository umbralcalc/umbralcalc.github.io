# How sustainable are freshwater fish populations in the UK?
<div style="height:0.75em;"></div>

In [this project](https://github.com/umbralcalc/anglersim) we build a simulation of freshwater fish populations and use it to evaluate the impact of environmental factors on their sustainability. The data used to train this model is described in the project repository.

Using the [stochadex simulation engine](https://umbralcalc.github.io/stochadex) we can define the simulation structure (based on a [Ricker population model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ricker_model)).
