# Data-driven woodland health simulations
<div style="height:0.75em;"></div>

- Goal: Build a woodland health monitoring app that monitors the spatial disease spread in UK woodlands over time and helps decide on the most important areas to help first.
- Datasets:
  - Forest condition survey: https://www.data.gov.uk/dataset/cccef1ac-fcd2-456a-89eb-49f7206e9ce1/forest-condition-survey-1987-2006
  - Forestry research time series: https://www.forestresearch.gov.uk/tools-and-resources/statistics/time-series/
  - OpenForest: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2311.00277
